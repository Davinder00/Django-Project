The Cemetery of Forgotten Books. Carlos Ruiz Zafón

The Cemetery of Forgotten Books is a captivating book series that takes readers on a journey through the winding alleys and dark corners of Barcelona. Set against the backdrop of Franco's Spain, these books tell the story of a secret library known as the Cemetery of Forgotten Books, which houses forgotten and neglected books of the past. Each book in the series follows a different character as they uncover the secrets of the Cemetery and the stories contained within its walls. With its intricate plot, vivid descriptions, and rich historical context, The Cemetery of Forgotten Books is a must-read for anyone who loves mystery, history, and a good book.

The Harry Potter series J.K. Rowling

The Harry Potter series written by J.K. Rowling, is a modern classic of children's literature that has captured the hearts and imaginations of millions of readers around the world. The series follows the adventures of Harry Potter, an orphaned boy who discovers that he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns to master magic and confront the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort. The series is known for its intricate world-building, memorable characters, and themes of love, friendship, and the power of good over evil. The Lord of the Rings J.R.R Tolkien

The Lord of the Rings is a classic high-fantasy novel series by J.R.R. Tolkien. It follows the journey of a hobbit named Frodo Baggins, who is entrusted with the task of destroying a powerful and malevolent artifact known as the One Ring. Along with his loyal companions, Frodo embarks on a perilous adventure filled with magic, danger, and legendary creatures. Set in a richly imagined world with intricate histories and cultures, The Lord of the Rings is a timeless epic that has captured the hearts and imaginations of readers for decades. Its themes of heroism, friendship, and the struggle against evil make it a must-read for fans of the fantasy genre and beyond.
